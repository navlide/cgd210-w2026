FAQ

Quick answers on sizing, care, shipping, and returns.

Is it waterproof? +

It’s designed to handle light drizzle and snow flurries. For heavy rain, layer with a dedicated rain shell.

How should it fit? +

Regular fit: comfortable over a tee or hoodie. If you’re between sizes and want a closer look, size down.

Can I machine wash it? +

Yes—cold wash, gentle cycle. Tumble low with clean tennis balls to restore loft (or air dry and shake out).

What’s your return policy? +

30-day returns and free exchanges. Try it on indoors, keep it clean, and you’re good.
